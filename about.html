Founded in 2025 by a small group of devoted animal lovers, Fremont Cat Rescue began as a humble foster-based initiative. What started with a single rescued litter has now grown into a network of more than 100 active volunteers, dozens of foster homes, and hundreds of successful adoptions each year.

Our passion stems from witnessing the daily struggles of abandoned and stray cats in Fremont and nearby neighborhoods. Today, we are proud to serve as a trusted bridge between vulnerable cats and loving forever families.
